What Does Depression Feel Like?

Depression is a complex and multifaceted mental health condition, and its experience
can vary from person to person. It's important to note that what follows is a general
description and individuals may experience depression differently. If you or someone
you know is struggling with depression, it's crucial to seek professional help.

Depression can be more than a constant state of sadness or feeling “blue.”

Symptoms of depression

  1. low mood
  2. oss of interest in activities
  3. hopelessness
  4. reduced libido
  5. fatigue
  6. changes in cognitive functioning
  7. sleep disruptions
  8. eating more or less

Different types of depression
